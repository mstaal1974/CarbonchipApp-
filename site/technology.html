Air-Curtain Engineering

Clean combustion, on every site.

A controlled air curtain forms a high-velocity barrier over the burn chamber, trapping particulates and reburning them through clean secondary combustion. The result meets strict regulatory smoke controls without the cost or carbon of hauling biomass to a fixed facility.

  • Particulate capture: Air-curtain traps and reburns smoke and fines.
  • Mobility: Track / trailer chassis reaches remote forestry coupes.
  • Fire-load reduction: Drastically lowers on-site fuel loads and ignition risk.
